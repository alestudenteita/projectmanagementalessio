Project Management Methodologies

Alessio's extensive training and certification in multiple project management methodologies enable him to employ diverse strategies tailored to project needs, ensuring efficiency and excellence.

  • PRINCE2® 2017 Practitioner: This methodology is widely recognized for its structured project management approach which divides projects into manageable and controllable stages. It is particularly valued for its flexibility and can be tailored to all types of projects.
  • Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM): As an entry-level certification offered by PMI, CAPM teaches fundamental project management principles and terminology. It is ideal for those starting a career in project management or those who manage larger projects.
  • Six Sigma Yellow Belt: This certification introduces individuals to the concepts of Six Sigma, including its techniques and tools. The focus is on improving quality and efficiency in operational processes through statistical analysis and quality control measures.
